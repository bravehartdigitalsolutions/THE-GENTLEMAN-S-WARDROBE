About the Brand

Built on Fit, Not Trends

The Gentleman's Wardrobe began as a single rail of hand-picked suits in Kampala, chosen for men who wanted to look assembled without saying a word. Today it's a full wardrobe — suits, shirts, footwear and the small details that finish a look.

Our Mission

To make premium menswear accessible without cutting corners on quality or fit.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted name in modern menswear.

2021
The first rail. Opened with a small curated selection of suits and shirts.
2023
Footwear added. Expanded into leather shoes, loafers and sneakers.
2025
Full wardrobe. Accessories, fragrance and personal styling introduced.
Today
Trusted citywide. Hundreds of professionals, grooms and students dressed.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom tailoring and fittings?

Yes — every suit and blazer purchase includes a complimentary in-store fitting, with adjustments typically ready within 24–48 hours.

Can I order and pay through WhatsApp?

Absolutely. Message us your size and preferred item, and we'll confirm availability, price and delivery before you pay — no surprises.

Do you deliver outside Kampala?

Yes, we deliver countrywide through trusted courier partners. Delivery within Kampala is typically same-day.

What if the size doesn't fit?

We offer free size exchanges within 5 days of purchase, provided the item is unworn and in original packaging.

Do you handle bulk orders for events or teams?

Yes — from wedding parties to office uniforms, we offer bulk pricing and coordinated fittings for groups.

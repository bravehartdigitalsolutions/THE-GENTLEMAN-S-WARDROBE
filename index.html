SEC.04 — ORIGIN

Built in Kampala, for every Ugandan who wants the real thing.

TechSphere Uganda started with one frustration: too many Ugandans were paying full price for devices that turned out to be clones, refurbished units sold as new, or phones with no real warranty behind them. We set out to build the opposite — a store where "genuine" isn't a marketing word, it's a receipt, a serial number, and a warranty card you can actually use.

MISSION
Make premium technology accessible, trustworthy, and locally supported for every Ugandan.
VISION
Become East Africa's most trusted destination for consumer electronics.
VALUES
Authenticity, integrity, speed, craft, community.
